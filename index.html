Common Inquiries

How early should we start the ACID filing?
We recommend starting at least 48 hours before the cargo departs the origin port to ensure the ACI number is generated and shared with the shipper in time.
Do you handle project cargo and heavy machinery?
Yes, we have extensive experience in specialized customs procedures for industrial machinery and large-scale project equipment.
What documents are required for Egypt importation?
Typically: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Certificate of Origin, Bill of Lading, and any specific permits required by GOEIC.
